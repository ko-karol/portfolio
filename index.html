How I work

From idea to launch.

  1. 01

    Understand

    I start by listening. What problem are we solving? Who for? What does success look like in three months? Good software begins with good questions.

  2. 02

    Design in code

    I prototype in the browser, not in Figma. Real interactions, real constraints, real feedback. This cuts design-to-dev translation loss to zero.

  3. 03

    Ship small, often

    Feature flags, incremental rollouts, observability from day one. I'd rather ship 80% to real users than 100% to nobody.

  4. 04

    Measure + iterate

    Data over opinion. I instrument everything, watch the numbers, and let users tell me what to build next.
